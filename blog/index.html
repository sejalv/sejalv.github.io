Bookshelf

Books I can recommend...

The Manager's Path

Camille Fournier

A Guide for Tech Leaders Navigating Growth and Change

Grounded in real-life scenarios of engineering culture, and comes with tactical advice for engineers stepping into leadership on handling challenges and managing people in Tech.

Never Split the Difference

Christopher Voss

Negotiating As If Your Life Depended On It

Written like a thriller, with practical negotiation strategies for everyday life, that focus on building relationships, emotional intelligence and tactical empathy.

Invisible Women

Caroline Criado-Perez

Exposing Data Bias in a World Designed for Men

Cleverly dismantles the systemic inequity with data, and demonstrates how that has quietly impacted almost every aspect of daily life.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman

On dual-system thinking, understanding cognitive biases in humans, and tools for improved decision-making.

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

Engaging storytelling that weaves together insights from history, science, and economics to reflect upon human nature, identity, and culture.
