Bookshelf

Books I can recommend...

The Manager's Path

Camille Fournier

A Guide for Tech Leaders Navigating Growth and Change

It's grounded in real-life scenarios of engineering culture, and comes with tactical advice for engineers stepping into leadership on handling challenges and managing people in Tech.

Careless People

Sarah Wynn-Williams

A Cautionary Tale of Power, Greed, and Lost Idealism

A critical account of Facebook's internal culture and decision-making practices, given by a former director of public policy at Facebook.

Invisible Women

Caroline Criado-Perez

Exposing Data Bias in a World Designed for Men

It cleverly dismantles the systemic inequity with data, and demonstrates how that has quietly impacted almost every aspect of daily life.

Dream Count

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

A Novel

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman

On dual-system thinking, understanding cognitive biases in humans, and tools for improved decision-making.

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

A Brief History of Humankind

Engaging storytelling that weaves together insights from history, science, and economics to reflect upon human nature, identity, and culture.
