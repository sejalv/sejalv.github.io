The Manager's Path

Camille Fournier

A Guide for Tech Leaders Navigating Growth and Change

It's grounded in real-life scenarios of engineering culture, and comes with tactical advice for engineers stepping into leadership on handling challenges and managing people in Tech.

Careless People

Sarah Wynn-Williams

A Cautionary Tale of Power, Greed, and Lost Idealism

A critical account of Facebook's internal culture and decision-making practices, given by a former director of public policy at Facebook.

Empire of AI

Karen Hao

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman
